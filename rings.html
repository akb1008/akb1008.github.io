Earrings/Heart Ring Directions

I made my Touch of Color Earrings, one of my easier projects, where I attached elegant purple beads to lobster clasps and rings with pliers. After completing this project I was able to use the extra wire I had available to construct a heart shaped ring and a spiral centerpiece which I then added to a necklace chain. To construct the heart ring, I simply shaped the wire into a heart shape and wrapped it around my finger to get the appropriate size.
